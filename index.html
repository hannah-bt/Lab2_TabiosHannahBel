Living my life isn't a bed full of roses, it's a narrow way full of thorns and snares. I grew up not so privileged, I never had fancy toys nor fancy dresses. I always had to choose practicality over what my heart desires. Despite all that, my parents did their best to sustain our family's needs. And that fueled the fire in me to strive for the better. As a student, I am very detail oriented. I always put my best in everything that I do. If anything scares me, it's failure. I have experienced it twice, and I didn't like it. As an individual, I have a strong belief that everything is written in the stars—if it's meant to be, it will be. In my 19 years of existence, the very important lesson I've learned is to trust God and never in my own abilities, because God will honor the deeds of the humble ones.
